Let's journey through the world of blockchain

Goose-Tech Consulting, LLC began as a YouTube channel by me, who received the name Goose during my time as a musician. The Goose-Tech YouTube channel was initially focused on my tinkering with different computer hardware, software, and everything in between. The channel then hit its stride amid the crypto mining hype during 2018, which allowed me to focus on cryptomining tutorials. I later pursued media advisory roles with several developing cryptocurrency projects. As of late, my focus has been on blockchain tech and the flurry of new software and ideas beginning to take root all over the world. Where will this new technology lead us? Take a journey through the land of blockchain with me.
